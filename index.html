AI Concepts Summary

Author: Tallal Tawil

LLMs

Large Language Models (LLMs) are AI systems trained on massive text datasets to understand and generate human-like language. They are widely used for writing, coding assistance, summarization, translation, and conversational interfaces.

Agentic AI

Agentic AI describes AI that can act toward goals by planning steps, making decisions, and using tools with limited supervision. Instead of only responding once, it can operate through multi-step workflows.

Multi Agentic AI

Multi Agentic AI involves multiple AI agents working together, often with different roles, to solve larger tasks. This approach improves scalability, specialization, and problem-solving through coordination.

Artificial Generative Intelligence

Artificial Generative Intelligence focuses on creating new content such as text, images, audio, video, and code. Its core value is generation and synthesis, not just analysis or prediction.

Super Intelligence

Super Intelligence is a theoretical AI level that surpasses human intelligence across nearly all domains, including reasoning, creativity, strategy, and scientific discovery. It remains a future concept rather than current reality.
